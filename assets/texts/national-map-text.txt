The Democratic Republic of the Congo is a tropical country, the capital, Kinshasa, lying 4 
degrees South of the Equator in sub-saharan Africa on the western side of the continent, 
bordering the South Atlantic Ocean. It bounds part of the Congo River Basin and is bordered to 
the East by volcanic mountain ranges and the African Rift Valley lakes. It has an area of
2,345,408 sq. miles and is the second-largest country in Africa.
