With its rainforests, savanna, wetlands, lakes and mountains, the Congo has one of the 
most diverse populations of plants and animals in the world. This includes many 
endangered species, such as the gorilla and bonobo. There are known to be more than 10,000
species of plant, 600 timber species, 280 reptile species, 400 mammal species and 1,000 bird 
species.
