Allens Swamp Monkey https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allen%27s_swamp_monkey#Description

The allens swamp monkey is from 45 to 60cm long, males weight up to 6kg, females
up to 3.5kg. It subsists on leaves and fruit as well as beetles and worms. It is
a social animal, forming groups of up to 60 individuals, which communicate with 
calls and gestures.
