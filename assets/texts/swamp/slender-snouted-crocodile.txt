Slender Snouted Crocodile https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mecistops

Slender snouted crocodiles are between 2.5 and 4.2mtrs long. They weigh bwtween 125
and 325kg. Males are significantly larger than females. They subsist on smaller
mammals, birds, turtles and aquatic snakes. They construct nests near the river banks
from mounds of decaying vegetation.
